Objective: Introduce Fogo major partners

Fogo is supported by a strong ecosystem of strategic partners. These organizations bring infrastructure, data, liquidity, and developer tooling to help Fogo thrive from day one.


Pyth Network :

Pyth is a leading oracle network that delivers real-time, high-frequency market data on-chain. On Fogo, Pyth provides the critical pricing infrastructure that DeFi apps and trading protocols need for accurate and reliable operations.


Ambient :

Ambient is an innovative DEX and liquidity engine optimized for high-performance blockchains. Integrated into Fogo, it enables the fastest perp DEX onchain.


Valiant:

Institutional trading. The fastest spot DEX in crypto.


Infinex:

The crypto everything app. Launching day 1 on Fogo.


Wormhole:

Wormhole is one of the most widely adopted cross-chain messaging protocols. It connects Fogo to over 30 blockchains, enabling seamless asset and data transfers between ecosystems.


Metaplex:

Metaplex is the standard protocol for NFTs on Solana, and will supports Fogo as well. It provides tools and smart contracts for creating, minting, and managing NFTs.


Goldsky:

Goldsky provides real-time indexing and data streaming infrastructure. On Fogo, it enables developers to access on-chain data instantly for dashboards, apps, and analytics.
